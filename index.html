Biography

I'm currently pursuing my PhD degree in Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Dallas, where I'm supervised by Dr. Mehrdad Nourani. My broad research interests include designing computational frameworks based on machine learning techniques to tackle research problems related to neurological disorders, such as Epilepsy.
